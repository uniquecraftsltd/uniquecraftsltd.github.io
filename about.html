Welcome to Builderz

25 Years Experience

At Unique Crafts Ltd, we believe that great homes are built on trust, attention to detail, and genuine craftsmanship.

We are a family-run business founded by two brothers who have spent more than 25 years building their experience across the construction and refurbishment industry — sometimes working independently, sometimes side by side, but always sharing the same values: quality, honesty, and pride in every project.

Our journey combines two complementary paths.

One of us brings an academic and professional background in architecture, civil engineering, site management, and digital project coordination, supported by industry qualifications including SMSTS, CSCS, and First Aid certification. Over the years, we have worked with a wide range of companies and projects, gaining valuable experience in delivering high standards safely, efficiently, and professionally.

The other brings decades of hands-on expertise developed through years of sound engineering, sport centers design & construction, practical work on construction sites, mastering the craft through real-world experience, precision, and dedication to quality workmanship.

Today, we have brought those experiences together to create something personal: Unique Crafts Ltd.

Our goal is simple — to deliver handcrafted, high-quality refurbishment and renovation services that stand apart from mass-produced solutions and rushed workmanship.

From bathrooms and kitchens to flooring, decorating, underfloor heating, and complete property refurbishments, we treat every home as if it were our own.

Based in Worthing and Brighton, we proudly serve homeowners, landlords, and property investors across West Sussex.

We work with homeowners, landlords, and investors, delivering reliable workmanship, clean execution, and attention to detail. Our focus is on quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction across every project, from small improvements to full renovations.

For us, construction is not just about completing a project. It’s about creating spaces that families can enjoy for years to come.
